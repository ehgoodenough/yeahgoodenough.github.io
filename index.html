Yeah Good Enough Hackathon 2015

July 17th-19th. 48 hours. Very casual.

A hackathon to encourage rapid prototyping and lean development.
Or rather, to encourage everyone to make something that is "good enough." It doesn't have to be perfectly optimized or perfectly designed. It doesn't have to meet all the expectations we had when we first dreamed up the idea. It just has to work, even if the prototype is quick and dirty and hacky; that prototype is worth so much more than the idea that only lives in our head.

When?

Friday, July 17th - Sunday, July 19th

Where?

Co.lab, 55 E Main St #105, Chattanooga, TN

Who?

Industry professionals and college students and weekend hobbyists alike. Developers, designers, entrepreneuers, technologists, artists, and more.
Anyone who has ever had awesome idea, but "never had the time" or "doesn't know enough" or whatever other excuses they've been tell themselves.

What?

Get together with other cool people to make cool stuff!
